Canadian Animals

This is a Mule Deer

There are 5 different type of deer in Canada.
Mule Deer can weigh up to 200lbs
In terms of weight and length (4ft to 7ft);
they are known as a medium sized deer.
They have large ears, like that of a mule
and a larger tail compared to White Tailed Deer

This is a Moose

The Canadian Moose is the largest member of the Deer family.
They can grow up to 2 meters tall and weigh as much as a small Trailer.
Moose are well known for their size and their giant antlers.
Their favorite foods are mainly located in marsh like areas,
there they like to spend their time getting big.

The White Tailed Deer

White tailed deer are the most abundant species of large herbivores in North America
They got their name because their butt is white with a tiny black tip on their tail.
White Tailed Deer are the most hunted out of the 5 types of Deer. Reason for this
is most likely due to their population and how tasty they are!
